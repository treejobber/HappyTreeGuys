How much does tree removal cost? +

Tree removal costs vary based on the tree's size, location, and complexity. A small tree (under 25 feet) may cost $300–$500, while large trees near structures can range from $1,000–$3,000+. Crane-assisted removals for extremely large or hazardous trees may be higher. We provide free, no-obligation on-site estimates so you know exactly what to expect — no hidden fees, ever.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Absolutely. Happy Tree Guys carries $2 million in general liability insurance and full workers' compensation coverage. Our lead arborists are ISA Certified (International Society of Arboriculture). We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request — and we recommend asking any tree service you consider for the same.

Do I need a permit to remove a tree? +

Permit requirements vary by city and county. Some municipalities require permits for trees above a certain diameter, especially heritage or protected species. Our team knows local regulations and will advise you on permit requirements during your free estimate. In many cases, we'll handle the permit process for you.

How long does tree removal take? +

Most residential tree removals are completed in a single day. Small to medium trees typically take 2–4 hours, while large or complex removals may take a full day. Crane-assisted jobs involving multiple trees can occasionally span two days. We'll give you a clear timeline during your estimate so you can plan accordingly.

What happens to the wood and debris? +

We haul away all debris as part of every job — branches, logs, and wood chips are all removed. If you'd like to keep the firewood, just let us know and we'll cut it to length and stack it for you. Wood chips from our chipping process are available for mulch if you'd like them for your garden beds.

Do you offer emergency tree service? +

Yes — we provide 24/7 emergency storm damage response. When severe weather hits, our emergency crews mobilize immediately. We prioritize situations involving trees on homes, blocking driveways, or near power lines. Call our emergency line anytime and you'll reach a real person, not a voicemail.

Will tree removal damage my lawn or landscaping? +

We take great care to protect your property. Our crews use ground protection mats for heavy equipment, carefully rig limbs to avoid dropping them on gardens or structures, and clean up thoroughly. In the rare case that lawn damage occurs from equipment, we'll discuss repair options with you before we start.
